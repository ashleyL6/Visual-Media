Course Description

Intro to Communication Research is a class that focuses on different methods and approaches to conducting research in the field of communication and media outlets. Students learn all about quantitative and qualitative research methods and sampling strategies, as well as how to conduct focus groups and carry out other experiments to generate their own findings.

Teacher's Bio and Interests

Katherine Haenschen is an accomplished woman in the field of Communication research. Specifically, she specializes in how social media influences political campaigns and voter turnout. She has conducted several different types of research and written academic reports that have been published over the last five years.

My Opinion

Intro to Communication Research can at times be extremely dull, but the class has taught me more than I expected. I can now put on my resume that I have conducted a focus group, I know how to conduct survey experiments correctly and I know how to use different types of software that are used for research in the real world. At times it's hard to pay attention when you're getting overwhelmed with information, but Dr. Haenschen does a great job at making sure students are understanding the material she's teaching.

Expected Grade

I expect to earn an A in this class.
